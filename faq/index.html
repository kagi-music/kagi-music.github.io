Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the difference between GP/GPX File?

    GP is the newest file from Guitar Pro 7. GPX is the older file from Guitar Pro 6.

  • How to download GP/GPX file?

    1. Just choose between GP or GPX and click . It will open a new window. Click I'M A HUMAN.

    2. Wait for 3 seconds then click GET LINK.

    3. Click the download button at the top right.

  • How to open GP/GPX File?

    There are many software to open the file. But I only know these software.

    PC

    • Guitar Pro
    • TuxGuitar

    Android

    • Guitar Pro
    • TuxGuitar

    iOS

    • Guitar Pro

  • Why don't you upload GP5 file?

    GP5 is an old file of guitar pro. I made the tab using Guitar Pro 7 or newer. When I export the tab to GP5, the tab will broken because it can't read the new feature of Guitar Pro.

  • Are these tab 100% accurate?

    Of course not. But I will do my best to make it. If you want 100% accurate, just contact the composer or the arranger of the song.
