FAQ

What is the difference between GP and GPX File?

GP is the newest file from Guitar Pro 7. GPX is the older file from Guitar Pro 6.

How to open GP and GPX File?

There are many software to open the file. But I only know these software.

PC

  • Guitar Pro
  • TuxGuitar

Android

  • Guitar Pro
  • TuxGuitar

iOS

  • Guitar Pro

How to download the file?

Just choose between GP or GPX and click it. It will open a new window. Click I'M A HUMAN. Wait for 3 seconds then click GET LINK.

Why don't you upload GP5 file?

GP5 is an old file of guitar pro. I made the tab using Guitar Pro v7.5. When I export the tab to GP5 the tab will broken because it can't read the new feature of Guitar Pro.

Is this tab 100% accurate?

Of course not. But I will do my best to make it. If you want 100% accurate, just contact the composer or the arranger of the song.
